Itachi Uchiha

(うちはイタチ, Uchiha Itachi) was a shinobi of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan who served as an Anbu Captain. He later became an international criminal after murdering his entire clan, sparing only his younger brother, Sasuke.

Dōjutsu

Explore the unique eye techniques of Itachi Uchiha.

Sharingan

The Sharingan is a Dōjutsu kekkei genkai of the Uchiha clan. It grants the user the ability to copy almost any jutsu they see, along with exceptional genjutsu (illusionary technique) and taijutsu (physical technique) prowess.

Mangekyō Sharingan

The Mangekyō Sharingan is an awakened form of the Sharingan. It grants the user devastatingly powerful jutsu specific to each eye, but overuse can lead to blindness.

Itachi Uchiha's Feats

The Sharingan allowed Itachi to see the flow of chakra, predict movements, as well as facilitate his use of genjutsu. Against other genjutsu users, he could turn their genjutsu against them.

Master of Genjutsu

Itachi was renowned for his unparalleled mastery of genjutsu, capable of trapping opponents in powerful illusions that could incapacitate or control them.

Tsukuyomi

Itachi's Tsukuyomi is one of the most powerful genjutsu techniques, allowing him to manipulate time and reality within the victim's mind, often leading to their psychological breakdown.

Amaterasu

Amaterasu, the black flames that burn anything in their path, was a testament to Itachi's formidable offensive capabilities. These flames could not be extinguished until their target was completely incinerated.

Susanoo

Susanoo is a gigantic, humanoid avatar made of the user's chakra which surrounds them and fights on their behalf, that has been handed down throughout all the generations of the Uchiha Clan. It is the strongest ability available to those who have awakened the Mangekyō Sharingan in both eyes.

Itachi's Susanoo

Itachi Uchiha's Susanoo was yellow in the manga; in the anime, its skeletal and armoured forms were red and its humanoid form was orange. In its humanoid form, its face was framed by locks of hair, with an appendage on its chin reminiscent of a Pharaoh's false beard. When its armour was formed, Itachi's Susanoo resembled a long-nosed tengu and it wore a pair of magatama earrings.
